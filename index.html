    • Frequently Asked Questions

    How can IPM coaching classes help preparation for Business Management, Hotel Management, Mass Communication, B.Com., BCA, and BA entrance exams?

    While there are numerous entrance exams for these courses, the test pattern is more or less the same. Most of these exams comprise four sections: Quantitative Aptitude, English, Reasoning, and General Awareness/Business Awareness. There is only 10-15% variance among the exams. In our IPM coaching programs, all these sections are covered.

    How to make the most of every IPM mock test?

    Analytics is at the core of your mock performance report card at Career Launcher. Analyze the paper thoroughly with the insight made available to increase your score in the next mock attempt.

    What is the marking scheme of the IPM Aptitude Test?

    For each correct answer, the candidate is awarded 4 marks. Every wrong answer carries a penalty of 1 mark.

    What is the structure of the IPM Aptitude Test?

    The IPM exam consists of 100 questions, to be answered within 2 hours. The paper has two sections:

    • Quantitative Ability (60 Questions)
    • Verbal Ability (40 Questions)
